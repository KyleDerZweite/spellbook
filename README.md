### Spellbook

**Spellbook** is your open-source, self-hosted solution for managing card collections and building decks. It's a non-profit project built by and for card game enthusiasts.

-----

### Features

  - **Card Scanning:** Quickly digitize your physical cards using your device's camera.
  - **Library Management:** Organize, search, and filter your entire card collection with a powerful digital database.
  - **Deck Builder:** Create, edit, and optimize decks with an intuitive, user-friendly interface.
  - **Multi-Game Support:** Designed with a flexible architecture to support multiple card games in the future.
